About us

Touchline FPL is a data and analytics company created with the aim of helping you to improve within Fantasy Premier League using data. We ignore the hype and luck, instead gathering tens of thousands of data points before using a specifically designed formula to produce the average expected points of every single player for each game week. Giving you a base squad that can stand the test of a full season.

On top of giving you the best scoring players we have also calculated the best value players, meaning you have more money to spend on the big hitters.
